Our Story

Promptaholics was born out of frustration. After spending hours tweaking prompts just to get a decent result from ChatGPT or Midjourney, we realized that the difference between a mediocre AI output and an extraordinary one is almost entirely in the prompt.

So we built the library we always wanted — a free, well-organized collection of expert-level prompts covering everything from viral TikTok hooks to cinematic Midjourney scenes, business strategy to Python automation.

Every prompt in our library is crafted to get results. Not generic filler. Not "write me a poem about X." Real, structured prompts that work across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Midjourney, Sora, Runway and more.
