Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Plain English summary: Promptaholics is a free AI prompt library. We use Google Analytics to understand how people use the site and display Buy Me a Coffee to accept voluntary support. We do not collect personal information, require accounts, or sell your data. Your saved favorites stay in your browser only.

1. Who We Are

Promptaholics ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website at promptaholics.com (the "Site"). We provide a free, publicly accessible library of AI prompts for tools including ChatGPT, Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, and others. The site is free and does not require registration or login.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the site. The following data is collected automatically:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and country-level location
  • Interaction data: Which prompts you view, copy, or search for — collected in aggregate
  • Technical data: IP address (anonymized by Google Analytics), referring URL, session duration

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We cannot identify you individually from this data.

3. How We Use Information

We use data to:

  • Understand which prompts and categories are most useful to our community
  • Improve the site's content, design, and performance
  • Detect and fix technical issues
  • Measure the site's overall reach and growth

We do not use your data for advertising targeting, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond running the site.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Our site uses the following cookies and storage technologies:

  • localStorage (your browser only): We store your dark/light mode preference and saved favorite prompts locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser and is not sent to any server.
  • Google Analytics cookies: Track anonymous usage statistics including pages visited, session duration, and general geographic location (country level). You can opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Google AdSense cookies: Google AdSense places cookies on your device to serve personalized advertisements based on your interests and browsing history. Third parties, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads or aboutads.info.
  • Stripe cookies: When you make a purchase on our store, Stripe (our payment processor) places cookies to facilitate secure payment processing and fraud prevention.
  • Firebase authentication cookies: If you sign in with Google to submit content, Firebase sets authentication cookies to maintain your session.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of our site.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data about your session including pages visited, time spent, and general location (country level). Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Analytics on our site is configured with IP anonymization enabled. We do not share Analytics data with third parties.

6. AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads. For more information, visit policies.google.com/privacy.

7. Prompt & Image Submissions

If you submit a prompt through the PA Agent or submission form, or submit an image to the Image Vault, we collect:

  • The prompt text, category, tags, and description you provide
  • Your optional name or handle if you choose to include it
  • Image files uploaded to the Vault (stored via Cloudinary and Firebase)
  • Your Google account display name and email if you sign in via Google to submit

Submissions are reviewed by the admin before being published. By submitting, you grant us a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to display your content on the site. Do not include personal, confidential, or sensitive information in submissions.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdSense — Advertising cookies and personalized ads — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and database for PA Agent and Image Vault — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Cloudinary — Image hosting for the Image Vault — cloudinary.com/privacy
  • Netlify — Site hosting — netlify.com/privacy
  • Cloudflare — AI API routing and Workers — cloudflare.com/privacypolicy
  • Google Fonts — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Brevo (Sendinblue) — Email marketing (when you subscribe to our list) — brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy
  • Stripe — Payment processing for our apparel store. Stripe collects payment card details and billing information directly. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy
  • Printful — Print-on-demand fulfillment for our store. When you place an order, your name, shipping address, and order details are shared with Printful to produce and ship your item. Printful's privacy policy: printful.com/policies/privacy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI model powering our PA Agent prompt generator — anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google Gemini — AI model for image search features — policies.google.com/privacy

9. Store & Purchases

When you make a purchase from the Promptaholics Store, the following data is collected and shared:

  • Payment data: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never see, store, or have access to your full credit card number. Stripe handles all payment security and PCI compliance.
  • Shipping data: Your name and shipping address are shared with Printful solely for the purpose of producing and delivering your order.
  • Order data: Order details (items purchased, size, color) are stored in our Stripe account for transaction records and in Printful for fulfillment.
  • Email: Your email address is used by Stripe to send order confirmation and by Printful to provide shipping tracking information.

We do not sell, rent, or share your purchase data with any party other than Stripe and Printful as described above.

Returns & Refunds: As all items are print-on-demand and made specifically for your order, we do not accept returns for change of mind. If an item arrives damaged or with a print defect, contact us within 30 days at promptaholics1@gmail.com and we will arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to you.

10. Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months by default. Your locally stored preferences (dark mode, saved prompts) remain in your browser until you clear your browser data — we have no access to or control over this data. We do not maintain our own database of user information.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Know what data we hold about you (we hold only anonymized analytics data)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking (use the opt-out link in Section 4)
  • Clear your locally stored favorites and preferences (clear browser data)

For any privacy-related requests, contact us using the details in Section 13.

12. Children's Privacy

Promptaholics is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, please contact us so we can address it promptly.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our site evolves. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when we do. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to check this page periodically.
