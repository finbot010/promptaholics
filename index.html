Frequently Asked Questions

What is Promptaholics?

Promptaholics is a free AI prompt library with over 1,671 structured, tested prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, Kling, Gemini, Flux and more. Every prompt is copy-ready — no account, no signup, no cost.

Is Promptaholics free?

Yes — completely free, forever. No subscription, no free trial, no credit card required. Every prompt in the library is available to copy and use immediately.

How many prompts does Promptaholics have?

Over 1,671 prompts across 18 categories including writing, coding, marketing, business, image generation, video, social media, education, and more. New prompts are added every week automatically.

Which AI tools do the prompts work with?

Every prompt is tagged by AI tool. The library covers ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, Kling, Gemini, Flux, DALL-E, Runway, Pika Labs, Stable Diffusion and more. Filter by tool to find exactly what you need.

What makes Promptaholics different from other prompt libraries?

Every prompt is structured using proven frameworks like CoSTAR, RISEN, and Chain-of-Thought — not just random text. The library also includes an AI prompt builder, an Image Vault with weekly AI-generated artwork, a Prompt of the Day, and ARIA — a multi-agent hub for advanced users.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Visit the library, browse or search, click copy, and paste directly into any AI tool. Zero friction from start to finish.
